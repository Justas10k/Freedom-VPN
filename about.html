Frequently Asked Questions

What is a VPN?
A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a service that allows you to securely connect to the internet and protect your privacy by creating a private network within a public network.
Is using a VPN legal?
Yes, using a VPN is legal in most countries. However, some countries may have restrictions on using VPNs, so it's important to check the laws in your country before using a VPN.
Can a VPN be hacked?
While no technology is 100% secure, a reputable VPN service with strong encryption and security features can significantly reduce the risk of being hacked or having your online activity monitored.
How does a VPN protect my privacy?
A VPN encrypts your internet traffic and hides your IP address. It also masks your location and can allow you to access content that may be restricted in your location.
